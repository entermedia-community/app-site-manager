Mayan Studies Certification Level I

This three month course will prepare you to interact directly with the Mayan culture with first hand experience. Weekly interactive workshops are held in Guatemala that students may attend in person or using Google Hangouts remote video conferencing with live English translation. Also includes optional two week intensive in lago Atitlan Guatemala and full video archives of past sessions.

Eduardo Tecun is indigenous Guatemalan, speaks fluently in three Mayan dialects, and has studied Guatemalan History in the University of San Carlos in Guatemala.

Eduardo is eager to share his knowledge of Mayan History and Culture and as the culimnation of the course he will introduce students to his extended family and demonstate cultural aspects such as making a Mayan Sacrifice of gratitude, washing clothes by hand in a pila, and making traditional Christmas tamales.

Students will also be shown around the Lago Atitlan pueblos, the market and museum at chichicastenango, and the famous Popol Buj manucript in The Chicago Museum of History. Enroll Now
